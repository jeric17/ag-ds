Grower Agtech Audit

In 2019 we streamlined our approach and combined the experiences and processes three of our members have had to offer the Grower AgTech Audit. We have found that typically only 10-20% the potential of current on-farm technology is being utilized due to lack of compatibility, support, training, integration or resources needed. The Audit builds a custom plan to help growers optimize their organizations through the efficient use of technology.

Using surveys, interviews, group discussions, ride-a-longs and other tools, we take an outside look into an operation to evaluate what tech is currently used and how to get more value from those resources. Next, we build a strategic 5-year technology implementation plan in-line with company goals. The result is a roadmap to follow providing recommendations and a framework, with measurable milestones along the way, that create actions and a company culture around innovation. Once we complete the audit, we sit down with the appropriate leadership to present our roadmap, discuss findings, suggestions and next steps. Our client now has the tools necessary to implement the plan internally or we can continue working with the team to manage projects and their implementation, further ensuring success.
